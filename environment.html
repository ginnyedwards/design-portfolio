This project was all about the environment, The Environment Keeps Happening To Me. We based this project around Bermondsey Spa Road Bridge, here we thought about ways in which we could intervene within the space creating an experience for people to take part in. We really wanted this intervention to focus on the senses specifically hearing. We created two pieces to have within the space that people could interact with. One was a trolley that used a Zoom recording device, recording the sounds that the movement of the trolley made on the floor and playing them back to the user in a set of headphones. For this piece we really wanted the sound to be the main thing that people were taking is so we provided blindfolds to the user. The other product use two large speakers that were connected to a piece of string. The string was attached to the pillars under the bridge and vibrated when the train went over the bridge, these vibrations were then played out of the speakers for the people passing under the bridge to listen to. In this project we really wanted to connect the user with the various noises that the bridge makes, the bridge is used constantly and by many people but we felt that most people disregarded it and never took the time to really see and hear it.

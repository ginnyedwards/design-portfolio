Modern London talk is a 16 stop audio guided tour around South London, on this tour you stop at 16 different Modern buildings, listen to the audio and spend time with the building. This tour is a tool to prove that Modernism is alive. People can go to these buildings and see the life that is being passed between them and the people surrounding them. The tour is my first tool in proving the life of Modernism but not my last, I hope to continue to add to it but also to continue to add more tools to keep Modernism living. The best way to keep something alive is to keep it with us, spend time with it and keep talking about it. This tour helps you to do that, you are encouraged to understand how you are proving live for the buildings and how the buildings are providing life for you and other people.
